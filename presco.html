Presco Appointment Booking

App Overview

Presco Appointment Booking simplifies booking medical appointments and conducting virtual consultations. With features like video calls and easy access to prescriptions, Presco allows users to connect with doctors from the comfort of their homes.

Key Features

  • Online Appointment Booking:

    Browse and book available slots with doctors.

  • Virtual Consultations:

    Enjoy real-time video calls with doctors.

  • Prescription & Information Sharing:

    Get prescriptions and medical advice after consultations.

  • Real-Time Notifications:

    Never miss an appointment with timely reminders.

  • User-Friendly Interface:

    Intuitive design for easy navigation.
