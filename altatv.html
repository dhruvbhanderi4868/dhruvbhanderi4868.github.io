AltaTV OTT Player

App Overview

AltaTV OTT Player allows users to stream movies and series online with a seamless and smooth video player experience. Just like other popular OTT platforms, it provides functionalities such as real-time watch time updates, easy switching between episodes in a series, and the ability to like or dislike content. Enjoy a smooth video player experience with the ability to seek forward and backward like Netflix and other leading streaming services.

Key Features

  • Stream Movies and Series:

    Watch high-quality movies and series directly from the app with an intuitive and smooth video player.

  • Like/Dislike Content:

    Rate content with a simple like/dislike feature.

  • Real-Time Watch Time:

    Keep track of your watch time with real-time updates.

  • Quality Changes:

    Quality changeable in all content with automatic episode changes.
