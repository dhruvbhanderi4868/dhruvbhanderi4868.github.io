Order Management - Personal Use

App Overview

Order Management is designed for personal use, helping you to manage and track your orders from pickup to delivery. With a barcode scanner, you can easily scan items during pickup and delivery, ensuring the process is quick and efficient. The app allows you to see all order details and track the progress of each order.

Key Features

  • Order Tracking: View all orders and their status at any time.
  • Pickup Management: Track orders that need to be picked up for delivery.
  • Picked Items Display: Once items are picked up, they are displayed as "picked".
  • Barcode Scanner: Use the built-in barcode scanner to scan items for both pickup and delivery.
  • Simple User Interface: Easy-to-use app with clear and organized details for efficient management.
