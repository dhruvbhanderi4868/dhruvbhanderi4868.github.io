Speech To Text

App Overview

Speech to text is a powerful and intuitive app that converts spoken words into accurate text in real time. Designed for efficiency and accessibility, this app is perfect for note-taking, transcriptions, and hands-free typing.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Speech Recognition: Convert speech to text with high accuracy.
  • Easy Note-Taking: Save and edit transcriptions effortlessly.
  • Multi-Language Support: Recognizes multiple languages and dialects.
  • Export & Share: Save transcriptions as text files or share them via email, messaging apps, and cloud storage.
  • Customizable UI: Personalize the interface for a seamless experience.
