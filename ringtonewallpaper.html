Ringtone Wallpaper

App Overview

Ringtone Wallpaper is your all-in-one customization app for personalizing your device with stunning live wallpapers and high-quality ringtones. With a vast collection of ringtones and wallpapers, you can easily set unique sounds and visuals for every occasion. Whether you want to set a specific ringtone for a contact, change your notification tone, or personalize your alarm sound, this app makes it effortless.

Key Features

  • Wide Collection of Ringtones:

    Choose from a variety of high-quality ringtones, including music, nature sounds, and classic tones.

  • Set Custom Ringtones:

    Assign specific ringtones to individual contacts for a personalized calling experience.

  • Alarm & Notification Tones:

    Customize your alarm and notification sounds to match your style.

  • Easy One-Tap Setup:

    Apply ringtones and wallpapers instantly with a single tap.

  • Favorites & Downloads:

    Save your favorite ringtones and wallpapers for quick access.
